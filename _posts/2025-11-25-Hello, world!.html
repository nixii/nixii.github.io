Welcome!

Welcome to my website! I am Nixii, and I am a programming & music nerd.

What is this website for?

This website is for anything I want to add. Posts will contain whatever pops into my head. I will publish here what I feel like should be. This is a personal website, after all.

You might notice that some of the posts on this site are not in English. These articles are most likely written in toki pona, a small and really fun language to learn. Sometimes I feel like expressing myself in it, therefore some of my posts will be written entirely in toki pona, and most likely will not have a translation. o pona!

How was this site made?

This site was created with the Jekyll framework. If you want to make a simple website similar to this, I cannot recommend Jekyll enough. It makes default page layouts and loading page posts a breeze. However, Jekyll only supports static websites, so if you want comments or user-generated content, it's not right for you. But for simple sites like this, it is great.
